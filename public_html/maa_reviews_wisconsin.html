The Martial Arts Shelf
December, 1999

Martial Arts America: A Western Approach to Eastern Arts  provides the novice martial arts student with a wealth of useful insights into a discipline that has often been obscured by mystery, mysticism, misinformation,, and marketing hype. After an informative introduction, experienced martial artist Bob Orlando explains why the martial arts are worthy of serious study, the evolutionary cycle of combat arts, the factors to consider with respect to preserving ancient martial art forms, the ethics of lethal force level skill development, the value of [martial art] tradition, the relationship of martial arts theory to that of martial arts practice, forms training (Kata), martial arts as sport, women and the martial arts, metaphysics and the martial arts, evaluation and selection of martial arts instructors, and much, much, more.  Martial Arts America is a super introduction for the novice, and holds much of value for even the experienced martial arts student and practitioner.
