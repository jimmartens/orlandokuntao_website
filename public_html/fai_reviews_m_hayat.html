First Principle of Fighting: Buy this Video
Mr. Orlando has accomplished a rare feat.  The ease with which this video relays the deep and hidden inner art of real and effective fighting methods is astounding.  Secrets of the arts not taught in the everyday dojo are uncovered with amazing ease and confidence.  The deadliest of techniques -- impaling, leg trapping, multiple points of contact -- you name it, it's here.  Traditional practitioners -- look behind the curtain and see your kata/form movements come alive.  Many black belts that have trained for a lifetime are not this effective and do not have this knowledge.

Bob Orlando is an excellent speaker, informative, and has the ability to demonstrate and break down complicated movements that even a novice will be able to understand.  If you're a serious martial artist that isn't afraid of what you don't know, you simply HAVE to buy this tape!!!
